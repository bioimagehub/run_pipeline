Step-translocation histograms

The step-translocation histograms are the bin distribution of distances for a given time-lag. As explained on Tracking session, the I-SPT translocation histograms may be corrected from tracking error (white bar).

Figure 1467. 

They also can be used to determine the number of different Brownian diffusing populations in the sample: 1 (do line), 2 (dash line or free (plain line).

Figure 1468. 

Correlation statistics
